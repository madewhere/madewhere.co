Dinnerware

Brands

Le Creuset

Le Creuset is a famous cast iron bakeware manufacturer. Probably best known for their Dutch ovens, but they make a considerable variety of kitchen products. While many of their cast iron products are made in France, the rest of their product lines vary in origin.
Vendors

Heath Ceramics

Heath Ceramics is primarily known for their ceramic products, but they also act as a vendor for a wide variety of items which appear to be from reasonable sources. They list origin of products they carry prominently on individual product pages.
Brands

Heath Ceramics

Heath Ceramics is primarily known for ceramic goods which they make in their California factory. They’ve recently started producing other products, like flatware. They also carry a significant number of other items which seem carefully selected, but haven’t been comprehensively evaluated. See their vendor page for more information.
Brands

Corelle

Corelle is well known for their distinctive glass dishware, which is made in Corning, NY.
Brands

Duralex

Duralex is a manufacturer of glassware; they specialize in drinkware and dinnerware, and is known for its robustness. All of their glassware is made in France, and can be bought direct from their USA distribution. In 2021 they were purchased by Pyrex, but while sharing a name with the American Pyrex (owned by Corning), Pyrex EU appears to be a different company.
