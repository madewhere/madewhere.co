Tanner Goods

January 29, 2022
This article is a stub. You can help improve it!

Tanner Goods is a leather goods manufacturer based in Portland, OR. They produce a range of belts, wallets, bags, and other accessories.

While Tanner’s core products are produced in the USA, they do not exclusively carry US-made products.
